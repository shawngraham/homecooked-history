A Demo Map

An interactive historical map showing how to add points, have them appear according to a timeline, and some other nifty things

Things to look at

Points have links to further info

Base layers can easily be added / switched on or off

The base data is date-of-foundation for various cities in the US and in Canada

Points are styled according to the type; points that do not have a recognized type do not get plotted

also georectified historical map base layers (zoom in on Ottawa)
