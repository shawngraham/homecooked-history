Image Sonifier

How it works

Upload an image to convert it into music! The app will:

  • Take three horizontal slices through your image
  • Convert brightness to pitch
  • Map red values to note duration
  • Map blue values to volume

You'll receive a MIDI file that you can open in any music software.
